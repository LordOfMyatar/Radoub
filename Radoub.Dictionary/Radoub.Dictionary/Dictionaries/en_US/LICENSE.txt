English (United States) Hunspell Dictionary
============================================

Source: LibreOffice dictionaries repository
URL: https://cgit.freedesktop.org/libreoffice/dictionaries/tree/en

This dictionary is based on a declassified NSA word list combined with
various other sources. It has been maintained by Kevin Atkinson and
is now part of the LibreOffice project.
